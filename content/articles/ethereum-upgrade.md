# Ethereum 3.0: The Future of Smart Contracts

The Ethereum Foundation has officially announced Ethereum 3.0, the next major upgrade to the world's leading smart contract platform.

## Key Features

### 1. Sharding 2.0

Enhanced sharding will dramatically increase throughput:
- 100,000+ transactions per second
- Parallel processing across shards
- Improved cross-shard communication

### 2. Zero-Knowledge Rollups

Native ZK-rollup support will provide:
- Privacy by default
- Reduced gas costs
- Better scalability
